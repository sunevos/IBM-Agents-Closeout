// Educational Content

Built for youth,
powered by IBM.

Short, creator-style videos designed for a Gen Z audience.

All educational content was produced as short, Gen Z creator-led videos, drawing from IBM SkillsBuild's existing content and expertise to make the topic relevant and accessible for a global youth audience. Each piece was designed to encourage learners to explore Agentic AI, connect it to their own context, and continue learning through IBM SkillsBuild. Video examples and links will be added here.

Course objective: Introduce youth to what AI Agents are and why they matter, keeping content high-level and accessible, serving as an on-ramp to deeper, credentialed learning through IBM SkillsBuild.

// Looking Ahead — 2026

The foundation is set.
What comes next?

With a proven track record and a highly engaged global youth community, here are three opportunities for the IBM x Goodwall partnership to go further in 2026.

Idea 01 — Expand Agentic AI
Deeper learning, recognized credentials.
IBM SkillsBuild expands existing Agentic AI modules and continues driving toward deeper learning, leveraging the new Credly partnership for industry-recognized credentialing that learners can display on LinkedIn and digital portfolios.
Idea 02 — IBM and Business Branding
Help youth build their professional identity.
IBM SkillsBuild launches a professional branding programme, helping youth understand how to build a personal brand and create an online presence that opens doors to real opportunities and career connections.
Idea 03 — IBM Industry Highlights
IBM becomes Goodwall's most versatile partner.
IBM SkillsBuild runs programmes across all Goodwall themes and verticals, showing how technology and AI are transforming industries, centered on key moments and IBM partners like L'Oreal, FIFA, and Adobe.
